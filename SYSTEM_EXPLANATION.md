## For Customers, Cooks, and Investors

## 🎯 WHAT IS FOOD4LOVE?

**Simple Definition:**
A mobile app that connects people who want home-cooked meals (Buyers) with professional cooks (Cooks) who come cook at your location.

Think of it like:
- **Uber** but for cooks (not delivery, the cook comes to your home)
- **Tinder** matching based on food preferences
- **Airbnb for experiences** (you book a private chef)

---

## 💰 HOW THE PLATFORM MAKES MONEY

### Revenue Stream 1: Platform Fee (Main Income)
- **When:** Every time a cook completes a service and gets paid
- **How much:** 10% of the service price
- **Example:** 
  - Service price: ₦1,000
  - Cook receives: ₦900 (90%)
  - Food4Love keeps: ₦100 (10%)

### Revenue Stream 2: Payment Processing Fees
- **When:** When buyers pay via Paystack
- **How much:** Paystack takes 1.5% (we don't keep this, but it's part of our ecosystem)
- **Example:**
  - Buyer pays: ₦1,000
  - Paystack fee: ₦15 (goes to Paystack)
  - Cook's account: ₦900 (they receive)
  - Platform keeps: ₦85 from our 10%

### Revenue Stream 3: Referral System (Growth)
- **When:** Users invite friends successfully
- **Cost:** We give ₦700 to referrer, ₦700 to new user (wallet credit)
- **Why:** Growth hack - costs us ₦1,400 per user but brings 2 active users
- **Payoff:** Those users make bookings, we earn 10% on each

### Revenue Stream 4: Premium Features (Future)
- Verified badge for cooks
- Featured listings
- Cooking classes booking
- Subscription for frequent buyers

## 👥 USER JOURNEYS

### 🔵 BUYER JOURNEY (Person wanting to eat)

**Step 1: Sign Up → Verification**
- Download app
- Create account with email
- **Verify email** (security checkpoint)
- Role select: Choose "Foodie" (Buyer)
- Add photos (profile picture)
- **Done** → Access discovery

**Step 2: Discover & Match**
- **Discover Page** shows all available cooks nearby
- Swipe right (like) → shows interest
- Swipe left (pass) → skip cook
- Each cook shows:
  - Name, age, photo
  - Specialty cuisine (Jollof Rice, Soup, etc.)
  - Price range (₦3,000 - ₦8,000)
  - Rating from previous buyers
  - "Party available" badge if they cook for events
  - Daily special (what they're making today)

**Step 3: Matching**
- If you like a cook AND they like you → **MATCH**
- You can now chat with them
- Matches visible in "Chats" tab

**Step 4: Service Agreement in Chat**
- You chat with cook in app only (no phone numbers/WhatsApp allowed)
- Message filter prevents sharing of contact info (safety feature)
- Cook proposes: "I can cook Jollof Rice for ₦5,500"
- You accept by checking "I agree to this service"
- Service status: **PENDING**

**Step 5: Cook Arrives & Cooks**
- Cook comes to your location at agreed time
- Cook prepares the meal
- Service status: **PREPARING**

**Step 6: Payment & Rating**
- Cook marks service complete: Status → **COMPLETED**
- You're taken to Rating page
- **You MUST pay before you can rate** (this ensures buyers pay)
- Click "Pay" → Opens Paystack payment
- Enter card/bank details → Payment processes
- **₦700 BONUS added to your wallet** (referral bonus)
- Now submit 1-5 star rating + optional comment
- Service done!

**Step 7: Wallet & Future Bookings**
- Your wallet shows: ₦700 bonus credit
- You can use wallet credit for next booking
- Referral bonuses stack up if you refer friends

---

### 🔴 COOK JOURNEY (Professional cook)

**Step 1: Sign Up → Verification**
- Download app
- Create account with email
- **Verify email** (security checkpoint)
- Role select: Choose "Cook"
- Upload photos (profile + food photos)
- Add cuisine specialty (Jollof, Soup, Seafood, etc.)
- Add price range (₦3,000 - ₦8,000 per booking)
- Add cuisines you can prepare (select multiple)
- **KYC Verification** (Must verify identity before chatting)
  - Upload ID, selfie, proof of address
  - Approval takes 2-3 minutes
  - Prevents fraud, ensures trust

**Step 2: Discovery by Buyers**
- Your profile appears on Discover page
- Buyers swipe right if interested
- You also swipe on buyers you want to cook for
- Mutual swipes = Match

**Step 3: Chat & Service Agreement**
- Chat with matched buyer (app only, no contact sharing)
- **You propose the service:**
  - "I'll cook Jollof Rice with sides for ₦5,500"
  - Buyer must accept by clicking "I agree"
  - Service created with status: **PENDING**

**Step 4: Cook the Meal**
- Service status changes to: **CONFIRMED** (buyer paid)
- Travel to buyer's location
- Cook the meal (status: **PREPARING**)

**Step 5: Mark Complete & Get Paid**
- When done, click "Mark Complete"
- Status → **COMPLETED**
- Buyer goes to rating page and pays
- You receive: ₦4,950 (90% of ₦5,500)
- Earnings tracked in your dashboard

**Step 6: Analytics & Earnings**
- View earnings dashboard:
  - Total earned this month
  - Services completed
  - Average rating
  - Breakdown by cuisine type
- Withdraw earnings to bank account

**Step 7: Streak & Reputation**
- Complete more services → Build streak (consecutive completions)
- Get better ratings → Rank higher in Discover
- Premium features unlock (at 100+ completions)

## 🔐 SECURITY & TRUST FEATURES

### **Message Filtering**
- No phone numbers allowed in chat
- No WhatsApp/Telegram links (keeps them in app)
- No bank account details
- No external links
- **Why:** Prevents scams, catfishing, fraud

### **Email Verification**
- Required before you can book or cook
- Ensures real people only
- Recovery mechanism if you forget password

### **KYC (Know Your Customer) - COOKS ONLY**
- Cooks must verify identity
- Upload: National ID, Selfie, Address proof
- Prevents fake cooks
- Takes 2-3 minutes
- Builds trust for buyers

### **Ratings & Reviews**
- 1-5 star system
- Optional comments
- Prevents fake reviews (one review per service)
- Buyer can only rate after payment

### **Service Agreement**
- Both must agree before service starts
- Clear terms: dish, price, date
- Dispute resolution if issues arise

5. **Admin Sees:**
   - Cook_earnings record created
